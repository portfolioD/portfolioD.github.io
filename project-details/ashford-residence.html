THE ASHFORD RESIDENCE

Concept

James and Betty Ashford's fondness towards artistic outlets, coupled with inspiration from the natural surroundings of Black Mountain, bring to life the design of their home. Warmth, creativity, and gathering are created through autumnal colors, natural textures and patterns. Their charming abode has vintage character and is a place of solace and respite.

Objectives

  • Objective: Design a single family house for a family of four, considering accessibility and universal design needs
  • Type: Residential Design
  • Location: Black Mountain, NC
  • Area: 3,000 SF
