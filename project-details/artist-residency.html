ARTIST RESIDENCY

Concept

The artist residency provides a dynamic space that encourages creative pursuits. With zones dedicated to dining, living, working, and private retreats, the design facilitates both collaborative and solitary activities. Emphasizing privacy where needed with thoughtful adjacencies, the residency supports a seamless flow for daily routines and artistic endeavors.

Objectives

  • Objective: Design an adaptive reuse firehouse as an artist residency for the owner and guest artists that serves their needs and fosters creativity
  • Type: undefined
  • Location: 159 E 87th St, New York, NY
  • Area: 5,000 SF
