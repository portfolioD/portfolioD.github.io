BRIGITTE BARDOT'S HOME

French Country embodies charm, elegance, warmth, and comfort. There is a juxtaposition of rusticity yet refinement. Through carefully planned and selected lighting and fixtures, M ademoiselle Bardot's home in the south of France becomes a place of relaxation as well as gathering. Lighting supports overall needs and tasks while enhancing the ambiance of her villa.
