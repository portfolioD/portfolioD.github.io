About me

I am passionate about creating beautifully purposeful spaces. My creative approach is informed by my enthusiasm for historic preservation, the history of architecture and interiors, and timeless, classic design. I also firmly believe in designing for the greater good through sustainable methods and practices. Upon completing further education and work experience in the profession, I plan to earn the NCIDQ Certification and become an ASID Professional Member. I completed my graduation from the New York School of Interior Design with an Associate in Applied Science (AAS) in Interior Design and a Basic Interior Design Certificate (BID). I have Allied Member membership from prestigous AMERICAN SOCIETY OF INTERIOR DESIGNERS, ASID.
